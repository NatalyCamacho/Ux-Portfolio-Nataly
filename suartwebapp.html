Stuart Logistics

Tool For Small Businesses Deliveries

Nataly Camacho, Nancy Zhao, Ale Segon, Ornella Fierro

Research

Brief

Stuart is a logistics delivery service, that offers pickup and drop off solutions for big companies, small businesses and individuals. For this project the scope was to give small and local businesses a simple tool so they can order a courier to pick up packages and deliver them to their end customers. We had 2 weeks to work on this project.

What The Interviewees Said

“All the information for deliveries I have it stored in a notebook”
”Most of my clients are reocurring”
”When we have a lot of orders it gets very complicated to keep track of the deliveries”

Flowershop owners and small businesses employees

Pain Point

There is not a total summary of the performance of the employees related with time off and following HR metrics

User Story

Problem Statement

Small business owners need a system where they can organize and oversee scheduled & on-demand deliveries so that they can increase efficiency and productivity

Prioritizing

Style Tile

For the style tile of this project we based it on the company colors of Stuart. Also since Stuart's client is a person novice in technology we used big icons with labels to make the navigation easier

Prototyping

The first designs of the web app were very traditional so the client requested to experiment a little bit more with the layout. Stuart wanted to be more user friendly
