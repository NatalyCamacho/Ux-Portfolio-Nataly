Traveling Hobbies

By Nataly Camacho

App designed for IOS and android to help active people know about new places where to practice their hobbies when they are traveling

Research

Hypothesis

Traveling is an inconvenience for people to practice their hobbies

From the Interviews

7 out of 7 interviewees practice active hobbies

Active hobbies consist of activities such as hiking, biking, camping, traveling, etc

People Enjoy Practicing their hobbies with close friends

They were not really thrilled by the idea of sharing their leisure time with strangers

What The Interviewees Said

“Traveling is not an inconvenience for my hobbies it’s actually one!”
”I would like to have more information about the places where I can practice my hobbies while traveling”
”I do research before traveling so I can enjoy my hobbies”
“Even though I searched online I had not so good experiences on the recommended places”
Active people were really interested in knowing new places where they could practice their hobbies while traveling

Insights

In order to organice the findings from the interviews an affinity diagram was created

The diagram was divided by:
  • Interesting quotes the users said
  • What I understood from their behavior and reactions to questions
  • The pain points the user mentioned
Conclusion:

People practice both active hobbies such as swimming, hiking, camping and passive hobbies such as reading, cooking and crafting

The users were more interested about being able to practice their active hobbies while they were traveling rather than focusing on passive hobbies which they felt could do anywhere.

Pain Points

After analyzing what the interviewees said these were the most notorious pain points

  • Hard to find places to practice their hobbies while traveling. Need help improving on their hobbies
  • There is not enough information about their hobbies. Location where they can practice them or where they can buy the necessary elements.
  • Users don’t want to waste their free time
  • Users want to discover new places where they can practice their hobbies
  • Users need more information about the places where they can practice their hobbies

Problem Statement

Physically active people need a way to have more information about new places where they can practice their hobbies so they can keep enjoying their lifestyle
